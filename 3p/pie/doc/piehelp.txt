PIE Editor Help

Move UP           keypad-8 (up)
Move DOWN         keypad-2 (down)
Move LEFT         keypad-4 (left)
Move RIGHT        keypad-6 (right)
HOME (upper)      keypad-5 (HOME)
HOME (lower)      Shift keypad-5 (HOME)
Move RIGHT to tab TAB
Move LEFT to tab  ESC
LEFT/RIGHT EOL    Shift keypad-6 (right)
Delete LEFT       BACKSPACE
Delete char       keypad-9 (DC)
Delete line       keypad-3 (DL)
Delete line perm  Shift keypad-3 (DL)
INSERT line       keypad-1 (IL)
INSERT on/off     keypad-7 (IC)
PUT temp buf      (white)
PICK (line)       (red)
Erase to EOL      ERASE
Quote next char   Ctrl-K
DIVIDE split line Ctrl-D
Word wrap (at)    Ctrl-W
Save and exit     Ctrl-E
Save-as and exit  ENTER <filename> Ctrl-E
Save              Ctrl-V
Quit              ENTER Ctrl-E

+PAGE (fwd page)  f1
+LINE (fwd line)  f2
GOTO (top, line)  f3
-LINE (bwd line)  f4
-PAGE (bwd page)  f5
+SEARCH           ENTER <string> keypad-0
+SEARCH next      keypad-0
-SEARCH           ENTER <string> keypad-.
-SEARCH next      keypad-.


Arguments:

ENTER, <arg>, <command>

e.g.

ENTER 3 DL        Deletes three lines
